About Myself

I am a highly dedicated, hardworking and flexible self-thaught programmer, who is looking for new challange where I can improve my skills and consistently strive to work to the best of my ability and always deliver professional standards.


After almost 10 years of physical training with last 3 years of preparing for bodybuilding competitions due to several injuries, I decided it was time to choose different way. I spent a lot of time thinking about what might be the next move for me. Do I want to just train other people as Personal Trainer or learn something new entirely.

That's when I decided to settle on something that has always had a great appeal to me, Software Engineering. I've always had a great interest in computers, especially being a gamer, and have always wondered exactly how everything works.

That's why I decided to sign for SkillsCity bootcamp to build my foundations in Software Engineering. Throughout the course, I have obtained skills in web developement using HTML,CSS and JavaScript but area I am definetely more intersted about is Back-end developing technologies and I am looking forward to the opportunity to apply my current and also gain new knowledge in this direction.

I am aware, 12 weeks is no where near long enough to learn any programming language in its entirity, but with the foundations set, I am eager to continue to develop my skills on my own.
